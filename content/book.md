Somewhere, somewhen, there is an arcade. It is larger on the inside than it has any right to be. A group of strangers find themselves unable to leave. The arcade has rules that no one fully understands. Attention appears to have weight. Faith has a way of leaving marks.

*The Play Never Ends* is a weird fiction novel about belief. About leadership and what it costs. About the communities we build and what participating in them does to us.

In this story, as in life, be careful about how you interpret events. It may reveal things about you that you aren't ready to know, and it may cause you to miss things hidden in plain sight.
